Maeterna Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 16, 2026

Maeterna is operated by Full Stack Collective, LLC ("Full Stack Collective," "we," "us," or "our"), a company registered in the State of Wyoming, USA. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share information when you use the Maeterna platform ("Maeterna," the "Platform," or the "Service").

By using Maeterna, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

1. What We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Account information: name, email address, and authentication data (including data provided via Google Sign-In).
  • Health logging data: information you choose to record, such as blood glucose readings, blood pressure readings, and related notes or timestamps.
  • Medical registration information (doctors only): registration/license number and phone number provided at sign-up.
  • Institutional affiliation information (doctors only): hospital/department affiliations or private practice details you provide.
  • Usage data: basic technical data needed to operate the Platform (e.g., login timestamps, device/browser information).

We only collect data you or your care providers actively enter into the Platform. We do not passively track your location, browsing habits, or activity outside the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used strictly to:

  • Allow you to log and view your own health data over time.
  • Allow you to share your health data with doctors or hospital departments you explicitly authorize.
  • Allow authorized doctors to view the data you've shared, for the purpose of providing care.
  • Operate, maintain, and secure the Platform.

We do not use your health data for advertising, marketing, profiling, or resale. We do not sell your data to third parties under any circumstances.

3. Who Can Access Your Data

You control access to your data. By default, your health information is visible only to you. You may grant access to:

  • Individual doctors you select, or
  • A hospital department (e.g., an Obstetrics & Gynaecology department at an affiliated institution).

You can revoke access at any time. Once access is revoked, the doctor or department can no longer view your data going forward.

Doctors on Maeterna self-attest their medical registration details; we display this information so you can independently verify a doctor's credentials before granting access.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored using Cloudflare's infrastructure (database and file storage). We rely on Cloudflare's security safeguards and access controls to protect data in storage and in transit.

Authentication is handled via Google Sign-In (Google OAuth), and/or other supported login methods. We do not store your Google password.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

You may delete your data and your account at any time from within the Platform. Deleting your account permanently deletes your associated account and health data from our systems. This action is irreversible — once deleted, we cannot recover your data.

If you delete your account, any data previously shared with and separately retained by a doctor or hospital (e.g., in their own clinical records) is outside our control and subject to that provider's own record-keeping obligations.

6. Geographic Scope

Maeterna is designed primarily for use in Trinidad & Tobago and may be used elsewhere in the Caribbean. Maeterna is not intended for use in the United States and is not designed or certified to meet U.S. HIPAA requirements.

7. Children and Adolescents

Maeterna supports patients of all ages, including adolescents, consistent with normal practice for reproductive and maternal healthcare. If you are a minor, we encourage use of the Platform under the guidance of a parent, guardian, or your treating physician.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data stored about you.
  • Correct inaccurate data.
  • Delete your data and account at any time.
  • Control and revoke sharing permissions at any time.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated within the Platform. Continued use of Maeterna after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, contact us at: hello@fullstackcollective.com
